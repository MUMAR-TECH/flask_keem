About KEEM Driving School

Building Confident and Responsible Drivers Since Establishment

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, professional driver training that empowers individuals with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to become safe, responsible drivers. We are committed to excellence in driver education and maintaining the highest standards of road safety in Zambia.

Our Vision

To be Zambia's most trusted and respected driving school, recognized for producing skilled, confident, and safety-conscious drivers. We envision a future where every student who trains with us becomes an ambassador of responsible driving on our roads.

Our Story

KEEM Driving School was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality driver education accessible to everyone in the Copperbelt Province. What started as a single instructor with a passion for road safety has grown into one of the region's most respected driving schools.

Today, we operate two modern training facilities in Luanshya and Mufulira, serving hundreds of aspiring drivers each year. Our success is built on a foundation of experienced instructors, comprehensive training programs, and a genuine commitment to student success.

We understand that learning to drive is more than just passing a test – it's about building confidence, understanding road safety, and developing skills that last a lifetime. That's why we've developed a holistic approach to driver training that combines theoretical knowledge with extensive practical experience.
